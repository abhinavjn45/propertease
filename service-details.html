Statutory Audit Readiness

Navigating the complex landscape of Housing Society audits requires more than just spreadsheets. Propert-Ease's Audit Readiness module automates the reconciliation of ledgers, tracks Section 79 compliance, and ensures that every historical meeting minute is digitally signed and archived for statutory inspectors.

Our platform acts as your society's "Digital Constitution," providing a single source of truth that mitigates risks of financial discrepancy. By integrating maintenance billing with an immutable ledger system, we transform the end-of-year audit from a multi-month struggle into a seamless digital handoff.

What We Provide

We provide a comprehensive framework that aligns with the MCS Act and RERA mandates. This includes automated billing, expense tracking, and a dedicated portal for your society's external auditor to review records without interfering with daily operations.

The Challenge

Traditional society management often suffers from fragmented records, manual data entry errors, and a lack of role-based continuity when committee members change. Propert-Ease solves this by providing a persistent, institutional memory for your community.
